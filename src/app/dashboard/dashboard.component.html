What is CITE ?

CITE’s PDGM in Technology Entrepreneurship is an advanced 11 month program divided across 3 phases. It is for tech entrepreneurs and tech minded individuals seeking to launch / scale their ventures or work in the entrepreneurial eco-system such as seed funds, incubators etc.

How is CITE useful ?

How is CITE useful ?

The program is designed to give CITEians a deep dive in various aspects of building and scaling a technology venture. CITEians also get incubation support from Sardar Patel Technology Business Incubator (SP-TBI) – an incubation initiative of Sardar Patel Institute of Technology that is supported by Department of Science and Technology, Govt. of India.

What is purpose of CITE ?

What is purpose of CITE ?

The 1 year PGDM will help entrepreneurs in developing their tech products, understanding new age technology and crucial aspects of running a venture such as finance, go-to market, capital raising, IP protection, law etc. through a hands on practical approach. The program will also facilitate investor, client and mentor linkages.
